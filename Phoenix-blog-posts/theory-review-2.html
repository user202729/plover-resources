The “or” Sound

Words ending in the sound of “or” are stroked OER: store /STOER, more /MOER, roar /ROER.

The “or” sound in all other instances, except the “ory” ending is stroked OR: sort /SORT (this word ends in the t sound, not the or sound), oracle /OR/K-L, caloric /KUL/OR/-K or /KU/LOR/-K.

Shortcut the “ority” sound by stroking ORT: sorority /SU/RORT, juniority /JAOUN/YORT.

One-stroke alternatives for common “ority” words: authority /THOERT, seniority /SOERT or /SAOERT, inferiority /FOERT or /FAOERT, priority /PRAOIRT, majority /JOERT or /JOIRT, minority /NOIRT, superiority /SPROERT.

The “ory” ending is stroked OIR: allegory /AL/GOIR, auditory /AUD/TOIR, category /KAT/GOIR, derogatory /DRAUG/TOIR, statutory /STAFP/TOIR, suppository /SPAUZ/TOIR.

Add the “ory” stroke to common briefs: congratulate = /GRAFPT, congratulatory /GRAFPT/OIR; contribute =/ KRIBT, contributory /KRIBT/OIR; appreciate = /PR-GS, appreciatory /PR-GS/OIR; exhibit = /KPIBT, exhibitory /KPIBT/OIR; separate = /SPR-T, separatory /SPR-T/OIR.
